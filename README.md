# 🌟 Mental Health Check-in Tool

A simple yet meaningful application designed to help anyone (students, professionals, or anyone struggling) prioritize their mental well-being through daily mood tracking, journaling, and encouraging messages.

## 🎯 Purpose

Mental health matters. Whether you're a student juggling exams, a professional dealing with work stress, or anyone facing personal challenges—this tool is here to help you check in with yourself daily and build a healthier relationship with your emotions.

## ✨ Features

### 1. **Daily Mood Tracker**
- Log your mood on a simple 1-5 scale using emoji-based selection
- Track mood patterns over time with visual insights
- See trends to understand what affects your mental state
- Quick mood check-ins that take less than 30 seconds

### 2. **Encouraging Messages**
- Receive personalized affirmations based on your current mood
- Daily motivational quotes tailored to your emotional state
- Supportive messages that remind you: "You're not alone in this"
- Growth-oriented suggestions for difficult days

### 3. **Basic Journaling**
- Simple, judgment-free space to express your thoughts
- Guided prompts when you don't know where to start
- Reflect on your day without pressure
- Access past entries to see your progress

### 4. **Additional Features**
- Mood history and analytics dashboard
- Weekly mood summary with statistics
- Quick self-care tips for different situations
- Privacy-first: All data stored locally on your device
- Clean, calming, and accessible user interface

## 🚀 Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (Vanilla)
- **Storage**: LocalStorage (client-side, completely private)
- **Design**: Responsive, mobile-first approach
- **No Dependencies**: Pure vanilla JavaScript for simplicity and speed

## 🛠️ Installation & Usage

### Quick Start (Easiest Way)
1. Visit the deployed site (or open `index.html` locally)
2. Start your first mood check-in right away
3. No installation, no sign-up required

### Local Setup
1. Clone this repository:
   ```bash
   git clone https://github.com/Yorme-Sauce/Mental-Health-Check-in-Tool.git
   cd Mental-Health-Check-in-Tool
   ```

2. Open `index.html` in your web browser
3. Bookmark it for easy access!

No build tools, no npm packages—just pure, simple functionality.

## 💡 How to Use

### Daily Mood Check-in (2 minutes)
1. Click the **"Today's Check-in"** tab
2. Select your current mood (😢 to 😄)
3. Receive a personalized encouraging message
4. Optionally add quick notes about what's on your mind
5. Click **"Save Check-in"**

### Track Your Patterns (Weekly)
1. Navigate to the **"History"** tab
2. View your mood trends and statistics
3. See which days you felt best/worst
4. Identify triggers and patterns
5. Use insights for better self-care planning

### Express Yourself (Journaling)
1. Click the **"Journal"** tab
2. Use the provided prompt or write freely
3. There's no right or wrong way to express yourself
4. Click **"Save Entry"** when done
5. Your entries are saved locally for reflection

### Discover Self-Care
1. Visit the **"Self-Care Tips"** tab
2. Find quick, actionable tips for different situations
3. Try one that resonates with you
4. Build your personal self-care routine

## 🆘 Important Resources

**This tool is meant to complement, not replace, professional mental health care.**

If you're experiencing:
- Suicidal thoughts or self-harm urges
- Severe depression, anxiety, or panic attacks
- Crisis situations that feel overwhelming

**Please reach out immediately to:**
- **National Suicide Prevention Lifeline** (US): **988** or 1-800-273-8255
- **Crisis Text Line**: Text **HOME** to **741741**
- **International Association for Suicide Prevention**: https://www.iasp.info/resources/Crisis_Centres/
- **A trusted mental health professional** (therapist, counselor, or doctor)
- **Local emergency services**: Call 911 (US) or your country's emergency number

You deserve support. Reaching out is a sign of strength, not weakness.
